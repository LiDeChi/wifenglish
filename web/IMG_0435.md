## 📚 Dialogues / Role-Play Scenarios

The following dialogues are structured as role-play prompts, featuring Customer and Seller/Salesperson interactions.

### 💬 Dialogue 1: Asking the Time
```markdown
Customer: What's the time now? (What time is it now?)
Seller: It's half past seven.
```

### 💬 Dialogue 2: Work Schedule
```markdown
Customer: When must you go to work/go off work?
Seller: I have to leave at 7 o'clock.
```

### 💬 Dialogue 3: Store Hours (Opening/Closing)
```markdown
Customer: When will you come to our shop?
Seller: We open at half past eight a.m., and close at six thirty p.m.
```

### 💬 Dialogue 4: Store Hours (Alternative Example)
```markdown
Customer: What time will you come to our shop?
Seller: Our shop opens at nine o'clock, and closes at five o'clock.
```

### 💬 Dialogue 5: Travel Time/Distance
```markdown
Customer: How many times does it take from Foshan to Guangzhou by car?
Seller: About 50 minutes.
```
