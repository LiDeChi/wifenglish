# Lesson 4: Meeting Customers / 接待客户

***

## Dialogue Examples

### Dialogue 1

**(The dialogue appears to be a role-play scenario in a showroom)**

```markdown
Customer: Hello, welcome to our shop. What can I do for you?
(你好，欢迎光临我们的店。有什么可以帮您？)

Salesperson: We have to see some coffee tables.
(我们想看看一些咖啡桌。)

Customer: Sure. Are you looking for a specific style?
(当然。您是在寻找某种特定的风格吗？)

Salesperson: What kind have you are looking for?
(您在找什么类型的？)

Customer: I'd like to see some rustic ones.
(我想看看一些乡村风格的。)

Salesperson: Okay, we have a few options here.
(好的，我们这里有几个选择。)

Customer: Thank you.
(谢谢你。)
```

### Dialogue 2

**(The dialogue appears to be a role-play scenario in a showroom)**

```markdown
Customer: Good afternoon, sir. Can I help you?
(下午好，先生。我可以帮您什么？)

Salesperson: We're looking for a dining table and chairs. This way please.
(我们在找一张餐桌和椅子。请这边走。)

Customer: Sure. Do you have any sets that are durable?
(当然。你们有任何耐用的套装吗？)

Salesperson: Yes, we do. We have several styles available.
(是的，我们有。我们有几种风格可供选择。)

Customer: I'm just looking.
(我只是看看。)

Salesperson: Please take your time.
(请慢慢看。)
```
