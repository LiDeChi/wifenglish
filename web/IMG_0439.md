## Dialogues (Role-Play Scenarios)

### 1. Dialogue 1
**Customer:** Do you have any other material?
*(Chinese translation not provided for this specific line)*

**Seller:** Yes, we have MDF plywood, oak and so on.
*(Chinese translation not provided)*

**Customer:** Show me your fabric sofa please.
*(Chinese translation: 请给我看看您的布艺沙发。)*

**Seller:** OK, this way please.
*(Chinese translation: 好的，请这边走。)*

***

### 2. Dialogue 2
**Customer:** How about this yellow sofa?
*(Chinese translation: 这个黄色沙发怎么样？)*

**Seller:** No, I like it.
*(Chinese translation: 不，我不喜欢。)*

**Customer:** What is this table made of?
*(Chinese translation: 这个桌子是什么材质的？)*

**Seller:** It's solid wood.
*(Chinese translation: 它是实木的。)*

***

### 3. Dialogue 3
**Customer:** We are looking for a dining room set, with six chairs and a table.
*(Chinese translation: 我们正在找一套餐桌椅，六把椅子和一张桌子。)*

**Seller:** Certainly. This is our best-selling set. It's very durable and stylish.
*(Chinese translation: 当然可以。这是我们最畅销的套装。它非常耐用又时尚。)*

***

### 4. Dialogue 4
**Customer:** Is it plywood?
*(Chinese translation: 是胶合板吗？)*

**Seller:** No, it's MDF.
*(Chinese translation: 不是，它是MDF。)*

***
