## Dialogue Role-Play Section 2: Specific Product Inquiry (Beds)

This section focuses on specific details about beds and bedding.

```markdown
**Customer:** What kinds of bed do you have?
**Salesperson:** We have single beds, double beds, king size beds, children's beds.
**Customer:** Which kind do you need?
**Salesperson:** I need a double bed. Can you tell me the specifications of this one?
**Customer:** This is a 1.8m x 2m bed.
**Salesperson:** And what's the price?
**Customer:** Yes, that's the price.
```
