### **4. Dialogue**
| Speaker | English Text | Chinese Translation/Context |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Customer | Could you show me the best quality cone? | (Asking for a high-quality item, possibly referring to a specific product part or material.) |
| Seller | This is our best quality cone. | (Pointing out the recommended product) |
| Customer | Some, this way please. | (Requesting to see something else/a different location) |
| Seller | This way please. | (Guiding the customer) |
