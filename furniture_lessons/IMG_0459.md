## <0xF0><0x9F><0xAA><0x91> Dialogues Section 2 (Bottom Left)

**Seller:** Would you like to make the order now?
你现在就要下单吗？

**Customer:** If I order today, how long can I get it?
如果今天下单，多久能到货？

**Seller:** Don't worry. We can deliver it in 3 days.
不用担心。我们可以三天内交货。

**Customer:** Oh, it will be 3 days.
哦，会是3天。

**Seller:** Just a moment. I'll check with our factory.
请稍等。我帮您查一下工厂。

**Customer:** OK. Let's make it now.
好的，现在下订单吧。

## 🚿 Dialogues Section 3 (Top Right)

**Seller:** What size do you need?
你需要多大的尺寸？

**Customer:** I need a large one.
我需要一个大一点的。

**Seller:** How about this one? It's very popular.
这个怎么样？它非常受欢迎。

**Customer:** Is it waterproof?
它是防水的吗？

**Seller:** Yes, of course. We have many colors.
是的，当然。我们有很多颜色。
