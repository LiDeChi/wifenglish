## Dialogues / Role-Play Scenarios

### 1. Delivery Time

```markdown
Customer: When do you need the container?
Seller: Maybe next week.

Customer: When can we deliver?
Seller: As soon as possible.
```

### 2. Goods Needed for Delivery

```markdown
Customer: When do you need us to deliver the goods?
Seller: We need the goods delivered.

Customer: What do you need to deliver the goods?
Seller: Please tell me what different goods we need.
```

### 3. Dimensions / Size

```markdown
Customer: When can you deliver the goods?
Seller: We are ready next week.

Customer: Is it possible?
Seller: Yes, it is possible.
```

### 4. Scheduling Delivery Date

```markdown
Customer: We need the delivery on Sept. 16th. Is it OK?
Seller: I am not sure, but we will try our best to do that.

Customer: We need the delivery on Sept. 16th. Is it OK?
Seller: I'm not sure, but we will try our best to do that.
```
