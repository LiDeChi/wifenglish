# Lesson 17: Balance (余额)

## Vocabulary Focus

| English Word | Part of Speech | Chinese Characters | Meaning/Context |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **balance** | [n] | 余额 | The remaining amount due. |
| **settle** | [v] | 交付 | To pay or finalize an account. |
| **receive** | [v] | 收到 | To get something (e.g., goods, money). |
| **delivery** | [n] | 送货/交货 | The act of transporting and handing over goods. |
| **buy** | [v] | 买 | To purchase an item. |

***

## Dialogues (Role-Play Scenarios)

### Dialogue 1: Checking the Balance

```markdown
Customer: How much is the balance? 余额是多少？
Seller: It's $0,000 yuan.
```

### Dialogue 2: Payment Timing

```markdown
Customer: When do I need to pay the balance? 我什么时候需要付剩下的钱？
Seller: You can pay on or before delivery. 您可以在交货前支付。
```

### Dialogue 3: Card Payment

```markdown
Customer: Can I pay the rest amount by card? 我能用卡支付剩余的款吗？
Seller: Sure,当然可以。
```

### Dialogue 4: Delivery Scheduling

```markdown
Customer: We will pay the balance on delivery, and we want to check the goods. 我们会在交货时付余额，并且想检查一下货物。
Seller: OK, sure. 可以帮您查一下货柜。
```

### Dialogue 5: Inspecting Goods

```markdown
Customer: Now the goods are settled and you have checked it. Could you please pay the balance now? 现在我检查了。请您支付余额吧。
Seller: No problem.
```

### Dialogue 6: Finalizing Payment

```markdown
Customer: I will pay the balance after I receive the goods. 我收到货后，再付余额。
Seller: Sure, you can pay by card. 您可以用卡结算。
```
