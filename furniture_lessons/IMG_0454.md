# Lesson 12 Price Negotiation

## Price Negotiation (二)

*(Note: The page contains role-play prompts and a sample dialogue for practicing price negotiation skills.)*

### Role-Play Prompts / Scenarios

These numbered points provide scenarios for practice dialogues.

**1.**
This is last price, we can't make it lower.
这是最后的价格了，我们不能再降价了。

**2.**
Your price is too high, we can't make it lower.
你的价格太高了，我们不能再降价了。

**3.**
This piece is very nice, but we can't negotiate the price.
这件东西很好看，但是我们无法议价。

**4.**
If this cost rice, we can't make it lower.
如果这个成本是米饭，我们就不能再降价了。

**5.**
The original price is 980 yuan, we can make it 784 yuan.
原价是980元，我们可以做到784元。

**6.**
If your order is big, we can give you 20% discount.
如果您的订单很大，我们可以给您打八折。

***

### Dialogue Example (Showroom Sales)

*(This section provides a sample dialogue between a Customer and a Seller.)*

**Customer:** Sir, please give me the last price.
先生，请给我最低价。

**Seller:** Sorry, this piece is already very low, we can't make it lower.
对不起，这件东西已经很低了，我们不能再降价了。

**Customer:** What about 150 dollars? Will we make container?
那150美元怎么样？我们可以做集装箱吗？

**Seller:** No, let's make a deal.
不，让我们谈个交易吧。
