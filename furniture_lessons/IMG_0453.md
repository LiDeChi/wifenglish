## 🛍️ Dialogue Role-Play: Wholesale Pricing and Discounts

### Dialogue 1
```markdown
Customer: What is your wholesale price? / 你批发的价格是多少？
Seller: 980. / 九百八十。
Customer: Can you give me any discount? / 你能给我打折吗？
Seller: We can give you 10% discount. / 我们可以给您九折。
```

### Dialogue 2 (Negotiating Quantity)
```markdown
Customer: How much is this? / 这个多少钱？
Seller: 800 yuan for each. / 每个八百元。
Customer: OK, special price for you. / 好，特价给您。
```
