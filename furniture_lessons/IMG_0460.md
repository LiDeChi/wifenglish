### Dialogues (Role-Play Practice)

#### Dialogue 1

```markdown
Customer: Please pay 30% deposit now, and the balance on delivery.
Salesperson: OK.
Customer: OK, no problem.
```

#### Dialogue 2

```markdown
Customer: Can I pay 200 yuan as the deposit?
Salesperson: Sure. We can take it.
Customer: Okay.
```

#### Dialogue 3

```markdown
Salesperson: How much deposit do I need to pay?
Customer: You can pay 1,000 yuan.
Salesperson: The advance money you need to pay is 1000 yuan.
Customer: OK. I will pay by card.
```

#### Dialogue 4

```markdown
Salesperson: We accept cash or by card OK.
Customer: I want to pay 1000 yuan.
Salesperson: The advance money you need to pay is 10000 yuan.
Customer: Oh, I will pay by card.
```
