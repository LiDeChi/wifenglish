## Dialogues (Role-Play Practice)

### 1. Showroom Location Inquiry

**Customer:** Where is your showroom?
*(我们的店在哪里？)*

**Seller:** Our showroom is on the 2nd floor, Block A, Row 2 in Nanhua.
*(我们的展厅在二楼，A座二排。)*

***

### 2. Dimensions and Delivery Inquiry

**Customer:** Where will you deliver?
*(你们在哪里交货？)*

**Seller:** We can deliver to your place.
*(我们可以送到您家。)*

***

### 3. Product Location Inquiry (Furniture City)

**Customer:** Which slice in furniture city?
*(家具城哪个区？)*

**Seller:** From the South Wing, Block A, Row 2 Dynasty.
*(从南翼二排，A座二排。)*

***

### 4. General Directions/Navigation Inquiry

**Customer:** How to get there?
*(怎么去那里？)*

**Seller:** Enter the South Wing of our shop on the left.
*(进入我们店的左侧南翼。)*
