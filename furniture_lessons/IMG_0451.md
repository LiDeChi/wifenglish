## I. Role-Play Dialogues (Showroom Sales)

### Dialogue 1

**Customer:** Thank you. This way please. You see our beds are fashionable.
(谢谢，请这边。您看我们的床很时尚。)

**Seller:** What's this bed made of?
(这是什么材质的床？)

**Customer:** It's the imported real leather and most of our products are exported to Middle [East].
(它是进口真皮，我们大多数产品都是出口中东。)

**Seller:** Can you customize the size for us?
(你们可以定制尺寸吗？)

**Customer:** Of course. And I am sure our products will be popular in your country.
(当然可以。我敢保证我们的产品在你们国家一定很受欢迎。)
