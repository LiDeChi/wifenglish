## 🛋️ Role-Play Dialogues (Dialogue Script)

The dialogue section presents a conversation between a Customer and a Seller.

### **[English Dialogue]**

```
Customer: We have dining chairs, office chairs and boss chairs.
Seller: I want a boss chair. What colors do you have?
Customer: We have blue, black and red.
Seller: I'd like a red one. What material is it made of?
Customer: It's all leather.
Seller: You can choose among leather, fabric or artificial leather. How much is it?
Customer: It's 200 RMB.
```
