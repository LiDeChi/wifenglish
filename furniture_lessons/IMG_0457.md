### Scenario 1: Factory Visit (Dialogue)

| Speaker | English Text | Chinese Translation |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Customer** | Where is your factory? | 您的工厂在哪里？ |
| **Seller** | It's in Leconge, Shanghai. | 它在上海的乐成。 |
| **Customer** | May I view your factory? | 我可以参观一下您的工厂吗？ |
