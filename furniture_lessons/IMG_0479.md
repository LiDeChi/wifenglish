## Dialogue 6: Payment Details (付款细节)

**(Role-play prompt)**

| Speaker | English Text | Chinese Translation / Context |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Customer:** | Can we discuss about the terms of payment? | 我们能讨论一下付款的条款吗？ |
| **Seller:** | Sure. Which way of payment do you prefer? | 当然可以。您喜欢哪种支付方式？ |
| **Customer:** | I usually prefer to pay by card or by T/T. | 我通常更喜欢用卡或电汇支付。 |
| **Seller:** | Okay, so if you want to pay by card or by T/T, before we deliver? | 好的，那如果您想用卡或电汇付款，是在我们交货前吗？ |
| **Customer:** | Sure. OK. So I will pay the deposit by card and then I'll arrange a T/T for next week. | 当然。好的。那我先用卡付定金，然后我安排下周的电汇。 |
| **Seller:** | No problem. So you pay 30% of the total amount now. It's 5,000 RMB. Please swipe the card here. | 没问题。那您现在支付总额的30%。是5000元人民币。请在这里刷卡。 |
