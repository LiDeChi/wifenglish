# 🛋️ Imagining a Showroom Dialogue / 想象逛家具店

***

## 📖 Dialogue Script (对话)

The dialogue is structured as a role-play scenario between a Customer and a Salesperson, presented bilingually.

**Customer:** I like your products. Can I visit your factory?
*(顾客：我喜欢你们的产品。我可以参观一下工厂吗？)*

**Salesperson:** Yes, of course. I can show you around.
*(销售员：当然可以。我可以带您参观的。)*

**Customer:** When can I come?
*(顾客：我什么时候可以来？)*

**Salesperson:** OK, tomorrow is fine.
*(销售员：好的，明天没问题。)*
