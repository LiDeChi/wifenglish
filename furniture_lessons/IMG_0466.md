## Dialogue Practice: Showroom Sales Dialogues (Bilingual)

### Section A (Top Right Dialogue Prompts)

*(Note: The dialogue prompts are structured as role-play scenarios.)*

**1.**
*   **Customer:** How many pieces in one carton?
    (顾客：一个纸箱里有多少件？)
*   **Salesperson:** 2 pieces in one carton.
    (销售员：一个纸箱里有两件。)

**2.**
*   **Customer:** Will we pack it in one carton?
    (顾客：我们能把它们装在一个纸箱里吗？)
*   **Salesperson:** Yes, we will.
    (销售员：可以，我们会。）

**3.**
*   **Customer:** Will you use sponge in packing?
    (顾客：你们会用海绵做包装吗？)
*   **Salesperson:** Yes, we will. Are they all used for the same purpose?
    (销售员：是的，我们会。它们都是用于同一个用途的吗？)
