### **Dialogue 1**

**(Role-play scenario: Visiting a showroom)**

```
Customer: Welcome to our shop. Can I help you?
Salesperson: I want to buy some office furniture.
Customer: What style do you like?
Salesperson: Modern style.
```

*(Note: The dialogue structure suggests the first line "Welcome to our shop. Can I help you?" might be a general greeting, but based on the flow, it seems the Salesperson is initiating the conversation in this specific instance.)*

---

### **Dialogue 2**

**(Role-play scenario: Discussing furniture styles)**

```
Salesperson: This way please. All these are very popular.
Customer: Will you please tell me some patterns, they are very popular.
Salesperson: Certainly. We have many designs.
Customer: You can buy a sofa?
Salesperson: Yes, we do.
```
