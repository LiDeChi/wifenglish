## Dialogues / Role Play Scenarios

### Dialogue 4
**Customer:** Please make sure the packing is no problem.
**Seller:** Sure, we will.

### Dialogue 5
**Customer:** How do you want to pack it?
**Seller:** In wooden case.

### Dialogue 6
**Customer:** Are the chairs KD packed? (KD = Knock Down)
**Seller:** Yes, we pack the chair and legs separately.
**Customer:** 是的，我們椅子和腿是分包的。 (Yes, we pack the chair and legs separately.)

### Dialogue 7
**Customer:** Is there anything for the packing?
**Seller:** Ordinary packing is free while special packing is chargeable.
**Customer:** 普通包裝是免費的，特殊包裝是要收費的。 (Ordinary packing is free while special packing is chargeable.)
