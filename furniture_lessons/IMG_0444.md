## Role-Play Dialogues (角色扮演)

### Dialogue 1

**Customer:** How much is your quantity? (你们的数量是多少？)
**Salesperson:** We have 150 sets. (我们有150套。)

### Dialogue 2

**Customer:** How many sets do you need? (你需要多少套？)
**Salesperson:** I will order 150 sets. (我将订购150套。)
