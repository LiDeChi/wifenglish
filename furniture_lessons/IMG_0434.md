### Time Phrases / Examples

*   **half [ha]:** 半小时 (30 minutes)
*   **quarter [qua]:** 刻 (15 minutes)
*   **past:** 过了... (after)
*   **to:** 到... (before)
*   **a.m.:** 上午 (morning)
*   **p.m.:** 下午/晚上 (afternoon/evening)
