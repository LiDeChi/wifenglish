## 🛋️ Role-Play Dialogues / Showroom Sales Practice

### Dialogue 1

| Speaker | English Text | Chinese Translation (Pinyin/Characters) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Seller** | What can I do for you? | 我能帮您什么？(Wǒ néng bāng nín shénme?) |
| **Customer** | I want to buy some office furniture. Can you show me around? | 我想买一些办公家具。您可以带我参观一下吗？(Wǒ xiǎng mǎi yīxiē bàngōng jiājù. Nín kěyǐ dài wǒ cānguān yīxià ma?) |
| **Seller** | Sure. Our kinds of chairs including boss chairs and folding chairs. | 当然。我们有各种类型的椅子，包括老板椅和折叠椅。(Dāngrán. Wǒmen yǒu gèzhǒng lèixíng de yǐzi, bāokuò lǎobǎn yǐ hé zhédié yǐ.) |
| **Customer** | Good. One of chairs including boss chairs and folding chairs. | 好。有各种类型的椅子，包括老板椅和折叠椅。(Hǎo. Yǒu gèzhǒng lèixíng de yǐzi, bāokuò lǎobǎn yǐ hé zhédié yǐ.) |
| **Seller** | Our kinds of chairs including boss chairs and folding chairs. | 我们有各种类型的椅子，包括老板椅和折叠椅。(Wǒmen yǒu gèzhǒng lèixíng de yǐzi, bāokuò lǎobǎn yǐ hé zhédié yǐ.) |
| **Customer** | Good. I want to buy this computer table. What is it made of? | 好。我想买这张电脑桌子。它是什么材质的？(Hǎo. Wǒ xiǎng mǎi zhè zhāng diànnǎo zhuōzi. Tā shì shénme cáizhì de?) |
| **Seller** | This one is made of particle board. | 这个是刨花板做的。(Zhège shì bàohuābǎn zuò de.) |
| **Customer** | Oh, it's very nice. How much? | 哦，它很漂亮。多少钱？(Ó, tā hěn piàoliang. Duōshǎo qián?) |
| **Seller** | It's 700 RMB. | 七百元人民币。(Qībǎi yuán rénmínbì.) |
| **Customer** | But this price is too high. How much? | 但是这个价格太高了。多少钱？(Dànshì zhège jiàgé tài gāo le. Duōshǎo qián?) |
| **Seller** | 600 RMB is lower than the wholesale price! | 六百元人民币比批发价还低！(Liùbǎi yuán rénmínbì bǐ pīfā jià hái dī!) |
| **Customer** | Okay, then we have deal. | 好的，那我们成交。(Hǎo de, nà wǒmen chéngjiāo.) |
